LeadsPro

Created by Mathias, Sabrina, Anabelle, and Loges, is a Ruby on Rails web application that streamlines lead management for real estate professionals.
It uses AI-driven sentiment analysis to assess lead quality, enabling users to focus on the most promising leads and optimize their time efficiently.
With intuitive lead organization and seamless lead addition, LeadsPro enhances the process of connecting with potential clients interested in property, helping users maximize their outreach and productivity.
